Latest updates and public information

News, announcements, and health updates.

The JPHA News page shares important updates about healthcare services, public health programs, infrastructure development, partnerships, and community health activities across Jiwaka Province.

This page can be used for official announcements, health notices, service alerts, media releases, and community awareness messages.

News Page Focus

  • Health updates: Public health notices and announcements
  • Service news: Hospital, rural health, and outreach updates
  • Partnerships: Government, church, and health partner support
  • Development: Health infrastructure and workforce progress
Featured Update

Strengthening healthcare communication in Jiwaka Province

JPHA continues to play an important role in improving public health communication, healthcare coordination, and service delivery updates for communities across Jiwaka Province.

On a live website, this section can highlight the latest official announcement, health campaign, service improvement, or public notice.

Purpose of the News Page

A News page helps the public stay informed about healthcare services, new developments, leadership updates, health campaigns, and emergency notices.

For JPHA, it also creates one central place where people can follow health updates that affect families, patients, health workers, and communities throughout the province.

News Categories

Main types of updates that can be published by JPHA.

Health Announcements

Public health notices, awareness campaigns, disease prevention messages, and emergency updates.

Services & Facilities

Updates on hospitals, rural health centres, aid posts, medical supplies, and service improvements.

Partnerships & Programs

News about partnerships with government, churches, NGOs, WHO, and other health organizations.

How This Page Can Grow

Over time, the News page can become a full public information hub for JPHA announcements, media releases, service alerts, and health awareness campaigns.

  • Official announcements: Leadership, governance, and service updates.
  • Service alerts: Temporary closures, referral updates, and emergency notices.
  • Health campaigns: Immunization drives and awareness programs.
  • Partnership stories: Health projects, training, and community support.

Important Publishing Note

Before publishing official news, all dates, names, statements, photos, and article details should be confirmed directly with JPHA.

Why This Page Matters

A News page helps JPHA communicate clearly with the public, share progress, and build trust with communities.

It also helps families, patients, health workers, and partners find important healthcare information in one place.
