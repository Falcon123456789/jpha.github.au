Leadership & Governance

Guiding healthcare services across Jiwaka Province.

The Leadership page introduces the people and governance structures responsible for guiding healthcare services throughout Jiwaka Province.

JPHA leadership supports healthcare planning, operations, public health priorities, accountability, and institutional development.

Leadership Overview

  • Board oversight: Strategic guidance and accountability
  • Executive management: Operational healthcare leadership
  • Governance: Planning, reform, and institutional strengthening
  • Public service: Supporting healthcare delivery province-wide

Purpose of Leadership

Leadership plays a vital role in strengthening healthcare delivery, improving healthcare systems, and supporting healthcare workers throughout Jiwaka Province.

Governance and executive management help ensure healthcare services remain accountable, responsive, and focused on community needs.

  • For communities: Builds trust and transparency.
  • For staff: Provides direction and operational support.
  • For partners: Supports collaboration and healthcare coordination.
  • For governance: Strengthens accountability and leadership systems.

Leadership Structure
