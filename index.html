About JPHA

Jiwaka Provincial Health Authority is responsible for coordinating and delivering healthcare services throughout Jiwaka Province.

JPHA works with government, churches, healthcare partners, and communities to improve healthcare access and strengthen provincial healthcare systems.

Learn More
  • Hospital services: Partnership with Kudjip Nazarene Hospital.
  • Public health: Immunization and disease prevention programs.
  • Rural healthcare: Supporting remote aid posts and clinics.
  • Health partnerships: Working with local and international organizations.
