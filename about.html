About Jiwaka Provincial Health Authority

Who we are and why our work matters.

Jiwaka Provincial Health Authority (JPHA) is the government health agency responsible for coordinating healthcare services across Jiwaka Province.

JPHA supports hospitals, rural health centres, aid posts, public health programs, and community healthcare outreach services.

About JPHA

  • Government agency: Provincial healthcare authority for Jiwaka Province
  • Established: Officially launched in 2019
  • Main referral hospital: Kudjip Nazarene Hospital
  • Coverage: North Waghi, Jimi & Anglimp South Waghi districts

Who We Are

Jiwaka Provincial Health Authority was established under Papua New Guinea’s Provincial Health Authority reform framework to improve healthcare delivery and strengthen healthcare systems across the province.

JPHA coordinates healthcare planning, hospital services, public health programs, staffing, and rural outreach activities throughout Jiwaka Province.

  • Provincial leadership: Managing healthcare delivery province-wide.
  • Healthcare services: Supporting hospitals, clinics, and aid posts.
  • Public health: Immunization, awareness, and disease prevention programs.
  • Community focus: Improving healthcare access for all communities.
